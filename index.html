Welcome to Techtonica!

The Mission

  1. Train local, low-income women and genderqueer individuals in web development and other tech skills.
  2. Help companies support the community and build more diverse teams.

The Problem

Though many tech jobs are available, huge income disparaties exist in top tech cities like San Francisco.

A desperate need for greater gender, race, and sexuality diversity also exists in the tech industry.
