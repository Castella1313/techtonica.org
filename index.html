Trial Workshops

Techtonica works with local organizations to conduct free, one-day workshops.

Recruiting

We filter for interest, understanding, team-playing, resilience, commitment, and responsibility by asking students to teach before inviting them to apply.

Partner Companies

In order to secure interns, partner companies fund the training through pre-paid recruitment fees. Students are aligned with mentors and with a company that will employ them as interns.

Training

Accepted students receive laptops, a living costs stipend, and childcare. They participate in six months of full-time training including lectures, building assignments, projects, and experienced feedback.
