Openings

Techtonica is a new nonprofit that offers women and non-binary, feminine-adjacent adults with low incomes free tech training, along with living and childcare stipends, then places them in positions at sponsoring companies that are ready to support more diverse teams. You can read more here.

Make an Impact as Our Apprenticeship Program Facilitator!

Techtonica offers a life-changing learning experience to participants. We care deeply about helping our apprentices become high-quality developers.

We are looking for a committed individual to facilitate our apprenticeship program. You will be responsible for preparing and implementing a training plan in line with current software standards. It will be fundamental to provide guidance and knowledge to apprentices in addition to helping them develop their personal and professional skills.

Our participants are Bay Area women and non-binary adults and 75%+ people of color. We are seeking an individual who can relate to the target audience on as many levels as possible.

The ideal candidate will be passionate about the cause and the position and have the ability to create a relationship of mutual trust. They will know how to manage a group and make learning an effective and meaningful process.

The goal is to cultivate the apprentices’ tech knowledge and professional development.

Responsibilities:

  • Work Monday-Friday 9am-6pm, ~1 weekday evening, ~1 weekend day per month
  • Continually improve training content (instruction, notes, summaries, assignments, schedules, etc.) and distribute in a flipped classroom
  • Provide hands-on learning that builds portfolios for participants
  • Stay up-to-date with full-stack JavaScript best practices
  • Develop and enrich professional skills and connections by attending seminars, conferences, etc.
  • Collaborate closely with the CEO and board on the program vision
  • Organize one-day workshops with local organizations
  • Collaborate on designing and implementing project-based curriculum with sponsors
  • Manage apprentices, volunteers, and mentors
  • Run the apprenticeship application process

What we're looking for:

  • A minimum of two years of full-stack web development experience
  • Proven experience and ability as an instructor
  • The ability to quickly familiarize yourself with Techtonica’s vision and all people involved (staff, participants, board members, volunteers, etc.)
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong moral values and discipline
  • Organization, commitment, and adaptability
  • Putting personal biases aside as needed
  • Understanding of socioeconomic experiences that our apprentices have faced
  • Being passionate and informed about social justice and diversity-and-inclusion-in-tech issues
  • An extrovert who is respectful of introverts’ needs
  • Ability to handle many different projects and tasks simultaneously and independently

See some of the subjects we cover here.

The interview process involves an interview (online or in-person), writing a lesson plan, pairing, and meeting board members. Please send a link to your LinkedIn profile, other personal page, or resume to info@techtonica.org if this role sounds like a good fit for you.


Make an Impact as a Board Member!

If you are a leader who cares deeply about compassionately empowering local neighbors with low incomes, join us! We are especially seeking well-connected, skilled board members who are passionate about diversity and inclusion and social justice and who can relate to the people we serve on multiple levels: 75%+ of our participants are people of color, and all of them are local women or non-binary, feminine-adjacent adults with low incomes.

You are willing and able to:

  • Regularly make time to attend and prepare for meetings—we currently have a board meeting every other Monday evening and a committee check-in the Monday evenings in-between. Be involved in the topics when you miss meetings.
  • Answer emails within two days and be available to give opinions on matters that pop up via Slack or text.
  • Determine and be informed about Techtonica’s mission, services, policies, programs, and events.
  • Make decisions efficiently and be open to others’ ideas.
  • Communicate clearly and respectfully.
  • Participate in fundraising.
  • Attend one workshop per year.
  • Speak to participants once per year.
  • Help organize one event per year.
  • Commit one new sponsor per year.
  • Donate or find someone to donate $1675 per year.
  • Build partnerships with 2 community orgs per year.
  • Lead or participate in organizational committees.
  • Be a Techtonica ambassador at events, speaking gigs, etc.
  • Act at all times for the benefit of Techtonica and its purpose.
  • Disclose possible conflicts of interest, abstain from voting when a conflict may exist, and avoid self-dealing activities.
  • Seek outside help such as lawyers, accountants, and other professional services as needed by Techtonica.
  • Ensure up-to-date personnel policies, complies with employment laws, and follows these policies and laws.
  • Consult or advise staff in your area of expertise.
  • Providing thought partnership to the CEO and staff.
  • Use modern, online tech tools such as Google Drive and Google Calendar to manage organizational needs.

If this sounds like a good role for you, please email us with your LinkedIn profile and a little bit about how you relate to the target audience.
